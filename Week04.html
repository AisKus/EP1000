Week 04

In week 04, we are taught to do computer-aided design 3D on Fusion360. This page will show you the exercise we did during class. It is an exercise for us to be familiarise with Fusion360 and the tools provided.

This are the side view of how the cup looks like. When doing the mug, I sketch then extrude the circle on a certain height. As for the handle, I sketch it separately, extrude it and fillet it to make it smooth handle. To make the mug hollow, I used the shell tool to make hollow at a certain height. Afterwards, I assembled both parts together.

Here are the clearer view from the top how the inner mug looks like after being shell out..
